Omelette

Description

An omelette is a dish you might order for breakfast or brunch â€” it's kind of like a folded pancake made of beaten eggs, sometimes with the addition of vegetables, cheese, or meat. A good omelet is a little bit fluffy, but firm enough to contain its filling.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs beaten
  • 1 teaspoon sunflower oil
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. season the eggs with salt and pepper
  2. heat the oil and butter in a frying pan
  3. wait until the butter is foaming
  4. pour the eggs into the pan
  5. Tilt the pan multiple times to spread the runny egg
  6. now you can add whatever you like e.g. grated cheese, ham or fresh herbs
