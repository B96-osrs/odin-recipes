Tuna Salad

Description

Tuna salad is a light and fresh comfort food classic. Made with a few simple ingredients such as canned tuna, mayonnaise, onion and celery, itâ€™s the perfect combination of creamy and crispy. Serve this in a sandwich, wrap it in lettuce, or stuff it in avocado halves for a healthy, throw-together lunch.

Ingredients

  • canned tuna
  • mayonnaise
  • red onion
  • lemon juice
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. put the canned tuna in a bowl
  2. add mayonnaise
  3. chop the red onion
  4. add lemon juice
  5. add salt and pepper
  6. mix everything
